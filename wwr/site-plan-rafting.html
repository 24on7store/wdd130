Overview

Purpose

The purpose of my website is to market our rafting venue to local customers to draw their attentions to Dry Oar Rafting, and to convince the visitors to choose Dry Oar Rafting over other rafting competitors. This site will provide information and be inviting those who visit the site. It will help Dry Oar Rafting build revenue. The information provided also help those visitors to feel comfortable to choose Dry Oar Rafting for their adventure needs. It will show former customers enjoying our rafting services.

Audience

Our audience is young people (women and men) from 16 to 50 years old who want to have fun and adventures during summer vacations. Marital status does not matter because those who are married, single, students or professionals are more than welcome. They can come in family or alone to enjoy the rafting. They can access the content of our web site from pc, tablets or smartphones.....
